Toon Tanks

Drive a tank through a maze full of enemy turrets in this game designed in Unreal Engine.

Gameplay


Toon Tanks is a third person shooter game where the player is in control of a cartoon tank. Surrounded by a maze of enemies, the player must navigate the battlefield and destroy all the enemy turrets before their tank is blasted to pieces.

Project Elements


Toon Tanks was designed in Unreal Engine with player actions, gameplay events and enemy tracking programmed in a combination of C++ and Unreal Engine Blueprints. The player pawns and projectile pieces were built from pieces provided in asset packs, and the map was developed with customized structures. The game also includes sound effects and explosion animations to enhance the gameplay immersion.
