About Me

Hello, I am an undergraduate student at Case Western Reserve University, pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science with a minor in Computer Gaming. My focus is on software engineering, and I am passionate about AI, game design, and VR technology.

From a young age, I have been fascinated by computer technology and enjoy tackling challenging and creative projects. My current work includes developing digital twin environments with Unity 3D and NVIDIA Omniverse, designing full-stack applications, and creating interactive games Unity and C#.
