Guillotine Game

Compete with an friend to score the most points in this JavaFX application based on the card game "Guillotine."

Game Overview

This Guillotine Game is JavaFX adaptation of the card game “Guillotine,” which is set during the times of the French Revolution. During play, a series of nobility are lined up for execution, and players compete to earn points by claiming the most important beheadings for themselves. A list of 20 cards from a deck of 40 are lined up in the center of the screen, and on their turns players have the option to alter the order of the list in a number of ways that may benefit their strategy. They then complete their turn by taking a card, giving the next player a chance to do their turn. Certain cards, such as Marie Antoinette, are worth more points, and others base their values on the number of cards of a certain type that are collected during play. The game ends once there are no cards left to draw, and the player with the most points is the winner.
