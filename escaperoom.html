Escape Room

Explore the interior of a mysterious spaceship and attempt
to break free in this game designed in Unreal Engine.

Gameplay


In this game, the player finds themselves stuck in a spaceship, and must explore their environment to find clues that will help them retrieve the keys to open the main gate. As they explore, they can pick up items, open doors, and activate pressure plates to help make their escape.

Project Elements


The Escape Room was designed in Unreal Engine with player actions and gameplay events programmed in a combination of C++ and Unreal Engine Blueprints. The game map was developed using a set of prefabricated assets from the Epic Games Marketplace, which was amplified by attentive lighting design and sound effects.
